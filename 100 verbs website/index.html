Neo-Prehistory

Humans have extended out capabilities by means of instruments. The extension of our capabilities is synonyous with the expansion of desires. The evolution of instruments is the evolution of desire. We dulude ourselves by equating evolution with progress towards sophistication; it is not only wisdom that evolves but also folly, cruelty, and deceit. Is there really hope ahead in this evolution?
